# MoodCoinFun (MCF)

MoodCoinFun (MCF) is a meme and entertainment-focused cryptocurrency that aims to reflect and aggregate the daily mood of its community. It is deployed on the Solana blockchain as an SPL token, with initial liquidity provided on Raydium.

## Concept

MoodCoinFun is centered on the idea that the mood and sentiment of people in the crypto community are major drivers of engagement. The project encourages community members to share their daily mood and market sentiment via social channels and community events.

These mood signals may be used to inspire:

- Mood-themed community events  
- Social media campaigns  
- Visual branding and meme content  
- Possible future utilities and integrations
